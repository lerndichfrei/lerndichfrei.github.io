Get active!

You have decided to be a part of Lern Dich Frei! – that is great!

How do you want to bring yourself in? Are you an expert for a certain topic and wish to spread the knowledge? Do you know someone who certainly should be known by others too? Do you have a topic that you wish to dig more into it, but so far you had never a platform? Now is the time!

Seize your opportunity: Write us in a few sentences what your Event/Workshop/idea is about.. It does not have to be a fully laid-out concept, just make a first step and write us, we will help you for the rest!

You can propose your idea by mail to 365@lerndichfrei.ch or directly on the platform Openki. Openki is an interactive web-platform with the ambition to facilitate a barrier-free access to education for everyone. We collaborate with Openki in order to connect our events with other initiatives.

After receiving your suggestions, we will meet up and develop more out of it. We will give you all the information, how an event is created, which infrastructure we can use and who you could approach. And then you are ready to start your event!

We are looking forward to receive your ideas, to get to know you, and to accompany you throughout all phases, from the event planning until the after-show party.
