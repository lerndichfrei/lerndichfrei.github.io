About Lern Dich Frei

“Lern Dich Frei!” is a project initiated by students of the University of Zürich. During a one year cycle, the project encompasses activities, events and workshops to a broad spectrum of subjects all assimilating the idea of a sustainable future.

Main objective of the project is not only to convey knowledge, but more to build a platform for experts, to broaden networks and to trigger inspiring and practical ideas forall days life.

During one year, weekly events will take place, covering a wide range of aspects in terms of sustainability. Usually the participation will be free of charge. The calender shows all events that are fixed at the moment - just check it out and use the opportunity to be a part! Check out the calender here.

Important: There is lots of room for more events and workshops, so you are welcome to play a part! Or you just have an idea or a topic that you would like to introduce to the public awareness, but don't know how? Lern dich frei will help! Check out how to start an Event here.
